PINK GIN

MIX NOTE COMMENTS
Cola 7 Pink gin with cola is a combination that might surprise you more than you think. It's really a good combination as the flavors combine well but it's true that the gin is less noticeable.
Juice 6 It's fine if you don't have anything else to mix it with but think you'll miss a lot of the gin taste and the beauty of this drink is basically the taste.
Energetic 7 For some reason the energy is phenomenal with pink gin and makes it taste very nice. Highly recommended.
Fanta 7 In this case the fanta does a very good job of preserving the taste of the gin and the combination is really good.
Tonic 9 This alcoholic drink was born to be mixed with tonic... It is by far the best combination since it does not waste its taste, it levels it up.
Alone 5 You can't drink it... Without mixing it doesn't bind at all.
