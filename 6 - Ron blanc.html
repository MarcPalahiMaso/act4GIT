WHITE RUM

MIX NOTE COMMENTS
Cola 8 White rum and cola is a combination with a very pleasant taste that leaves you wanting more... Its taste is both smooth and sweet. It really is one of the best ways to combine white rum.
Juice 9 Personally, the times I've had the best time drinking have been with the combination of juice and white rum... This comes in alone, and it really is a taste that goes together very well.
Energetic 7 Energy drinks are also a great combination with this drink... Now, be careful that it rises very quickly but the taste is great.
Fanta 7 The fanta also looks great and it really is a very tasty and very good combination... I recommend it without a doubt.
Tonic 5 It's deadly...
Alone 5 White rum itself is a strong drink that, even if it doesn't have as much alcohol as others, is hard to swallow... Mix it up, it's not worth.
